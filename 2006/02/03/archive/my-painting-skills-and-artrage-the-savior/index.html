My painting skills (and ArtRage, the savior!)

2006-02-03

My lack of skill at painting/drawing is famous. I was terrible at art in school. My geography teacher would confuse my maps for fauna and my biology teacher would confuse my plants for countries. So, I joined art school in 6th grade - where after 1 class, I was put in with the 1st graders! :( I was so disappointed that I just quit - and since then have had to suffer being the cause of much mirth at Pictionary.

But then - bing - I discovered Art Rage a couple of days ago. Now, I dont know painting, and I know less about painting tools (layers, palette, canvas - what are those things?!) - but with Art Rage, I got started in a snap and in 15 minutes on my Tablet PC created what I consider a master piece.

I like the tool so much that now I’m spending a lot of time just doodling and experimenting with color - oils, crayons, sketch pens and pencils, all in Art Rage. I may become an artist after all :) The thing I love best about the tool is how well the oil paints mix!
