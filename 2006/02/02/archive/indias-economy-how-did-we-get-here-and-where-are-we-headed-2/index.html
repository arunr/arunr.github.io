India's economy - how did we get here, and where are we headed?

2006-02-02

An IMF working paper, “India’s pattern of development: What happened, what follows“ traces the history of India’s economic policies since indepence in 1947 and looks ahead into the future.

The paper is a dense 70 pager, and I’m ploughing through it - this is not one for a quick scan.

(Special interest: Raghuram Rajan, one of my professors at the Indian School of Business and now the Economic Counselor at the IMF, is one of the authors on the paper.)
