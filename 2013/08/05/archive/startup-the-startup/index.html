Startup the startup!

2013-08-05

Arijit (arijit@ptotem.com, managing partner of Ptotem Learning Projects LLC) & I conducted a workshop called “Startup the startup” at the Indian School of Business, Mohali Campus on 3rd & 4th August.

It was a complete blast - we worked with 18 students and took them from an idea to having a working prototype, 4 real customers, specs & mocks for the next version of the product, a pitch deck and the opportunity pitch to an investor - in less than 24 hours!

Here’s the deck we used to kick-off the workshop:

[iframe src=”http://www.slideshare.net/slideshow/embed_code/24925478“ width=”476” height=”400” frameborder=”1” marginwidth=”0” marginheight=”0” scrolling=”no”]

The templates we used in the workshop - the min spec template, the min pitch template and the min feature list template - are shared here. For mocks, we recommended the fantastic Bootstrap mockup template (here) or the Power Mockup add-in.

Both Arijit & I had a fantastic time working with the students - we were blown away not only by the effort and dedication put in by the students (exams got over on Friday, there was a party on Friday evening, the workshop was all of Saturday and half of Sunday, with some tough assignments to follow on Monday!) - but also by the quality of the thinking and execution of the idea.

Let us know your thoughts - and if you’re thinking of starting-up, we can summarize our workshop into 2 words - GO DO!
