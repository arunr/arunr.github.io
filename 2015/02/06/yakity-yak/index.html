Poetry

Yakity Yak

2015-02-06

Let me hold you tight
Through the night
My trembling child
From your dark and wild
dreams.

You looked so sad
That I wish I had
The power to steal
Your thoughts, to feel
your pain.

I wish you’d smile
It’s been a while
Since I gazed
On that radiant face
of yours.

What ails you now
I don’t know and how
I pray you’ll be
Without misery
or grief.

I want you back
Just yakity-yak
You and me
So happy and free
like before.
