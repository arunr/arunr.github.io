Looking forward to UnBox!

2011-02-21

I am going to the UnBox Festival in Delhi from 24th - 27th Feb, and I can’t wait for it! There are some excellent talks, workshops & partner events planned for UnBox, and it’s looking really cool. The detailed schedule for the event is posted here.

What are the things I’m looking ahead to?

  • Hearing what John Thackara & MP Ranjan have to say about sustainable development, design thinking and design education.
  • The talk on “Five Steps to Designing Classrooms for Real Learning” - I am deeply curious about the design of education (K & I were chatting just yesterday about whether education is really useful at all - beyond the basics of reading, writing & arithmetic!).
  • The “Crafting Livable Cities” session, a theme that K & I have spoken about so much.
  • The “Basics of Film Making” workshop.
  • The awesome partner festivals: EyeMyth, Beat Repeat, Technodrome and Salon der Alchemisten!
  • All the interesting people I’m going to meet!!
    I am going to be blogging & posting pictures from the conference - only 3 days more to go!
