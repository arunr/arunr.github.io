Four gadgets I want this year

2011-02-21

Four gadgets I want this year:

  1. Arduino: Cheap, open source hardware to build robots!
  2. MakerBot: Cheap, open source, 3D printing. Check out the great “Why I love my 3D printer“ talk!
  3. Samsung Beam: A phone with an in-built pico-projector.
  4. Motorola Atrix: A powerful phone, with a docking station that transforms it into a laptop! Read this NY Times article on “A Phone That’s the Life of a Laptop“ which reviews the Atrix.
