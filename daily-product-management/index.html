Daily Product Management

Daily Product Management

2013-08-29

I’m going to write a series of posts on product management, called “Daily Product Management”. The (incomplete) list below are the “pieces of wisdom” I used to mentor product management - and I’m going to write about each of these and link to the post. I will use the tag #pm for these posts, stay tuned!

  • Customers, customers, customers, customers.
  • Go for lunch with your devs & test.
  • Jump in and get started. Inertia is your enemy.
  • Features, Time and Resources are variables. Quality is not a variable.
  • Write specs. This is the bread and butter of the job.
  • Use your product daily.
  • Things are the way they are for a certain reason.
  • Change things. But not too much!
  • Relax!
  • Write clear email.
  • Triage daily.
  • Customers don’t see the feature you didn’t ship.
  • Customers do see the bug you didn’t fix.
  • Schedule for test.
  • Hire great people.
  • Be an evangelist.
  • Keep shipping.
  • Pick up the phone and talk!
  • Get out of the ivory tower.
  • You wont ship anything that doesn’t have a crunch at the end.
  • You don’t own the schedule - the dev & test own the schedule.
  • A team is as happy as the PM on it.
  • Stay up late. It’s your baby.
  • Build a network.
  • Keep your eyes and ears open.
  • Negotiation 101 - upfront & forthright.
  • Find a mentor.
  • Think Meta.
  • Do code reviews.
  • File great bugs.
  • Do hallway usability.
  • Write great status reports.
  • Get off the team the day you don’t get excited telling people about your product.
  • Your admin is your new best friend.
  • “Let’s ship the #!@$* product!
  • Have fun - don’t take yourself too seriously.
  • Get interested in people.
  • Read widely.
  • Learn to appreciate the arts.
  • Forward progress.
  • Get things done.
  • Do many things sufficiently well.
  • Design consistency.
  • R.E.S.P.E.C.T
  • Close your laptop during meetings.
  • Find time for side projects.
  • Why walk when you can run?
  • Short to-do list of 5. No more, no less.
  • Work hard. Rational things will happen.
  • My favourite animals? The ferret and the badger!
  • Escalation is not failure.
  • Dont let your email control you.
  • Learn from everyone.
  • First depth, then breadth.
  • Sink your teeth into problems, and don’t let go.
  • Go to work early on Mondays.
  • Reject NIH.
  • Is your schedule on the wall?
  • Don’t do email before going to bed.
  • Open and respectful.
  • Throw energy at the problem. Therein lies the solution.
  • ASK. It works.
  • Email -> Email Ping -> Phone Call -> In their office
  • Learn to set up processes that work without you.
  • If you work on Sunday, your week will end on Thursday.
  • Learn to say “I don’t know”
  • Learn to say “No”
  • Kindergarten manners: Sorry, Please and Thank you
  • A phone call is worth a thousand emails
  • If you get the right people in the room, the problem will be solved.
  • If they tell you it’ll take 2 weeks, they can do it in 15 minutes. If they like you.
  • You’re a super-hero!
